Introduction

Tidbit is a user-driven platform created for medical professionals and researchers have more digestible exposure to medical papers. Tidbit is a frontrunner in creating a centralized platform for medical professionals and researchers alike in creating visually engaging, peer-reviewed content.

Tidbit has two main features:

Creator Tool
 The Creator Tool™ is an in-house design tool where users can design their cited and peer-reviewed infographics to summarize medical papers. With a Creative Commons License and the unique ability to link the source Abstract to the infographic, Tidbit has helped researchers stand out in the age of high volume publishing.

Tidbit Feed
 The Tidbit feed is a collection of user-generated Tidbits shared by industry professionals with their peers. With the Tidbit feed, researchers and medical professionals alike can connect with each other and discuss their work. Using the Tidbit Feed platform has proved effective in helping researchers stand out, with an average of 203% increase in visual abstract views compared to a text abstract view.

Stakeholder Interview

After creating the current site map, I conducted a stakeholder interview with the CEO and product lead to confirm my understanding of the product and to discuss existing pain points that they have already identified. Some of the pain points mentioned by the stakeholders, based on user feedback and Google Analytics results were:

Current Customer Pain Points

1. Difficult to understand what the product is
2. Cannot sign up for product without .edu email
3. Cannot find Creator Tool

Problem Space

After understanding what the customer pain points are, I translated them into Problem Statements for the Tidbit Platform redesign. This way, we have spaces we can work in to better define our action items and goals.
